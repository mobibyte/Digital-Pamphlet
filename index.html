About

We are a club that allows members to expand their skills and knowledge in coding! Overall laidback environment, nothing too serious. Open to all students, regardless of skill level or coding experience. Collaboration is welcomed! No membership fees

What does MOBI do?

We primarily aim for web development, but we also have done app development from time to time! With enough resources, me may be able to host other events outside of social codings!
